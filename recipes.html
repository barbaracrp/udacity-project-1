Easiest Pizza Dough. Ever!

Aug 2

Ingredients:

wheat flour 300g

sugar 4g

salt 2g

yeast 4g

water 180g

olive oil 1 tbsp

How to:

Mix all dry ingredients with a fork in a large bowl.

Add the water, and make a dough using the hands.

Cover with a clean dishcloth.

Let it rest at least one hour.

Open the dough with a cilindric tube.

Add the toppings that you like.

Bake at 450F for 15min.
